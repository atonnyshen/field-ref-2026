Day 9 青年世代核心團隊訪談作戰包 (Nagaloka)

時間：2026 年 6 月 8 日 受訪者：Bhanu (巴努) 與 Sangharsh (桑格什) 核心角色：龍世學園 (Nagaloka) 新一代核心營運團隊

這是一份針對接下龍世學園重擔的「青年世代」所擬定的訪談大綱。他們擁有頂尖學歷，扛著巨大的生存壓力與創辦人的期待，在現代社會的洪流中試圖守護並延續安貝卡博士的人間佛教願景。

💡 主題一：世代交替與方法論的轉變 (Generational Shift in Methods)

Q1. 從啟蒙到體制化作戰

[EN] Lokamitra and Vivekratna's generation relied heavily on building grassroots faith and conducting retreats. As the new core team equipped with advanced degrees (like your MSW from TISS), how do you approach Dr. Ambedkar's mission differently? What modern tools do you use that the pioneers didn't have?

[TW] 世友居士和 Vivekratna 那一代，主要依靠建立基層信仰和透過閉關來啟蒙大眾。作為具備頂尖學歷（如 TISS 碩士）的新一代核心團隊，你們推進安貝卡博士使命的方式有何不同？你們使用了哪些開拓者當年沒有的現代工具？

💡 延伸提問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Can you give a specific example of a time when the founder's traditional approach conflicted with your modern administrative approach? How did you resolve it?
  • [TW] 您能否分享一個具體例子：創辦人過去的傳統做法，與您們現代的管理方式產生衝突時，最後是如何解決的？
  • [EN] Does professionalizing Nagaloka sometimes feel like it distances you from the raw emotion of the Dalit movement?
  • [TW] 將 Nagaloka 「專業化、體制化」的管理，會不會有時讓您們覺得，自己與達利特運動最初那種粗獷、強烈的草根情感產生了距離？

Q2. 生存壓力與純粹奉獻的拉扯

[EN] Vivekratna mentioned yesterday that younger people today face immense financial pressure to support their families, making it difficult to commit to the "careerless" volunteer life that the founders lived. How do you and your peers balance modern survival and professional ambition with a deep commitment to this social movement?

[TW] Vivekratna 昨天提到，現在的年輕人面臨巨大的養家財務壓力，很難像創辦人那樣過著「無生涯規劃」的純粹奉獻生活。您和您的同儕，是如何在現代生存壓力、專業野心，與對這場社會運動的深厚承諾之間取得平衡的？

💡 延伸提問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Have you or your peers ever thought about leaving Nagaloka for high-paying corporate jobs? What made you stay?
  • [TW] 您或您的同儕是否曾動過離開 Nagaloka 去企業賺取高薪的念頭？最後是什麼力量讓您們選擇留下來？
  • [EN] How does the pressure to support your parents and family affect your emotional well-being here?
  • [TW] 必須扶養父母和家庭的經濟壓力，如何影響您們在這裡工作時的心理狀態？

💡 主題二：教育、階級與真實世界的衝擊 (Education & Grassroots Reality)

Q3. 菁英教育與草根現實的對接

[EN] You represent the new wave of highly educated Dalit youth. When you return from elite academic institutions to run Nagaloka, how do you bridge the gap between academic theories (like structural poverty) and the lived reality of the grassroots students coming from 26 different states?

[TW] 你們代表了受過高等教育的達利特青年新浪潮。當你們從菁英學術機構回到龍世學園工作時，你們是如何在學術理論（如結構性貧窮）與來自 26 個邦的基層學生的真實生活困境之間，建立起溝通橋樑的？

💡 延伸提問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Do students ever look at you as "elites" who don't understand their local struggles? How do you break that barrier?
  • [TW] 學生是否曾把您們當作「不懂基層疾苦的菁英」？您們是如何打破這種隔閡的？
  • [EN] What is the most common misconception that academic textbooks get wrong about the actual situation of Dalits in the villages?
  • [TW] 關於村莊裡達利特人的真實處境，學術教科書上寫的理論，最常在哪裡出錯或脫離現實？

Q4. 現代社會中的勞動尊嚴

[EN] At Nagaloka, everyone must participate in manual labor like cleaning the campus and toilets to practice absolute equality. In modern India, where many educated youths desire comfortable desk jobs, how do you guide your students to find dignity in manual labor and realize that it is a crucial part of spiritual liberation?

[TW] 在龍世學園，所有人都必須參與打掃校園與廁所等日常勞動，以實踐絕對的平權精神。在現代印度，許多受過教育的年輕人都渴望舒適的辦公室工作，您們是如何引導學生在這些親力親為的勞動中看見尊嚴，並體會到這也是精神解放的一部分？

💡 延伸提問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Have you had students refuse to clean toilets because they felt it was below them, or because it triggered past caste traumas? How did you counsel them?
  • [TW] 是否有學生因為覺得「這不是讀書人該做的」，或者因為這勾起了過去種姓制度下的創傷，而拒絕清理廁所？您們是如何引導和說服他們的？
  • [EN] How do you personally set an example in these daily chores?
  • [TW] 在日常的勞動中，您防們自己是如何以身作則的？

💡 主題三：營運現實與佛教慈悲的交鋒 (Administrative Reality & Buddhist Metta)

Q5. 財務自立與核心精神的平衡

[EN] As the core team handling Administration and Income Generation, you face the immense pressure of running this large campus. We know traditional donations are no longer enough. What innovative ways are you trying to make Nagaloka financially self-sustaining, and how do you ensure you don't lose the core Buddhist spirit in the process?

[TW] 作為負責行政與創收的核心團隊，你們面臨著營運這個龐大校區的巨大壓力。我們知道傳統的捐款已經不夠了，你們正在嘗試哪些創新的方法來讓龍世學園達到財務自立？在這過程中，又如何確保不失去佛法的核心精神？

💡 延伸提問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] If Nagaloka charges fees or creates commercial streams, does it risk alienating the poorest students? How do you prevent this?
  • [TW] 如果 Nagaloka 開始收費或發展商業化項目，是否會面臨把最貧窮的學生拒之門外的風險？您們如何防範這一點？
  • [EN] Are there disagreements with the older board members regarding how to generate income?
  • [TW] 對於如何增加收入，您們與老一輩的理事會成員是否有過意見分歧？

Q6. 科技時代的雙面刃

[EN] Vivekratna compared modern smartphones to a "drug" that distracts youth. Yet, your generation must leverage digital technology and social media for organization and outreach. How do you utilize these tools for your mission without losing the focus and mindfulness required for Dharma practice?

[TW] Vivekratna 將現代智慧型手機比喻為讓年輕人分心的「毒品」。然而，你們這代人必須利用網路科技來進行推廣和組織。您如何利用這些工具推進使命，同時又不會讓學生失去佛法修行所需的專注與正念？

💡 延伸提問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] How do you manage smartphone usage among students during their residential stay at Nagaloka?
  • [TW] 在學生的寄宿期間，您們是如何管理學生的智慧型手機使用時間的？
  • [EN] What digital campaigns have you initiated that successfully connected with Dalit youth outside the campus?
  • [TW] 您們發起過哪些數位推廣活動，成功地與校園外的達利特青年建立連結？

💡 主題四：世友居士的遺產與認可 (Lokamitra's Legacy & Validation)

Q7. 「被看見」的渴望與自我期許

[EN] In our casual chat, I noticed you asked if Lokamitra mentioned you in our interviews. It shows that even as you independently run this campus, the acknowledgment from the founder still means a lot. As you take on this heavy burden, what do you most want to prove to Lokamitra? What do you hope he sees in your generation?

[TW] 在我們閒聊時，我注意到您順口問起「世友居士有沒有提到我？」。這讓我感受到，即使你們已經是獨當一面的管理核心，創辦人的「看見與認可」對你們依然意義重大。在承接這份重擔的過程中，你們最渴望向世友居士證明什麼？希望他能在你們身上看到什麼特質？

💡 延伸提問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Do you ever feel the shadow of Lokamitra's immense legacy is too heavy to walk out from?
  • [TW] 您是否曾覺得世友居士留下的巨大身影與遺產，沉重到讓您們難以走出屬於自己的路？
  • [EN] If Lokamitra were to give you one piece of praise, what would you want it to be?
  • [TW] 如果世友居士能給您們一個讚許，您最希望他讚許您們哪一個方面的努力？

Q8. 與創辦人世代的意見相左

[EN] Every generation has disagreements with the one before it. Looking back at the decisions made by the pioneer generation (Lokamitra, Vivekratna), what is one strategic decision you deeply respect, and one approach that your younger team is actively trying to change?

[TW] 每個世代都會與前輩有意見分歧。回顧開拓者世代（世友、Vivekratna）所做的決定，哪一項戰略決策是你們深感敬佩的？又有哪一種做法或思維，是你們年輕團隊現在正積極試圖改變的？

💡 延伸提問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] How do you handle discussions when the founders disagree with your new proposals? Do you back down, or how do you persuade them?
  • [TW] 當創辦人不同意您們提出的新提案時，您們通常如何處理？是選擇退讓，還是有特定的說服策略？
  • [EN] In what ways do you think the founders' era is fundamentally different from today's political/social landscape in India?
  • [TW] 您認為創辦人當年的時代背景，與今天印度的政治與社會形勢有什麼本質上的不同？這如何影響決策？

💡 主題五：未竟之業與未來願景 (Unfinished Business & The Future)

Q9. 校友網絡的真實挑戰

[EN] The "Alumni Network" now spans across India. From your perspective managing this on the ground, what is the most brutal or realistic challenge these alumni face when they go back to their home states, and how are you practically supporting them?

[TW] 「校友網絡」現在遍布全印度。從你們這些在第一線管理網路的世代來看，這些校友回到家鄉後，面臨的最殘酷或最現實的挑戰是什麼？你們目前是如何實質地支援他們的？

💡 延伸提問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Have there been alumni who faced direct physical danger or severe social boycott when they tried to start social projects in their villages? How did Nagaloka intervene?
  • [TW] 是否有校友在試圖於家鄉村莊啟動社會計畫時，面臨直接的身體威脅或嚴重的社會排擠？Nagaloka 是如何介入協助的？
  • [EN] How do you keep the scattered alumni motivated when they feel isolated in remote areas?
  • [TW] 當校友在偏遠地區感到孤立無援時，您們是如何維持他們的熱情與向心力的？

Q10. 寫給三十年後的備忘錄

[EN] If you look 30 years ahead—when your generation has fully taken the helm and you are the senior elders—what is your ultimate vision for Dr. Ambedkar's "Prabuddha Bharat"? What is the one "unfinished business" of the pioneer generation that you are most determined to complete?

[TW] 如果放眼三十年後——當你們這代人完全掌舵、成為資深長老時——您對安貝卡博士「覺悟的印度（Prabuddha Bharat）」的最終願景是什麼？世友居士那一代有哪一項「未竟之業」，是您下定決心一定要完成的？

💡 延伸提問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] If you could leave a message to the next generation of Nagaloka leaders who will succeed you, what would it be?
  • [TW] 如果您能給未來將接替您們的下一代 Nagaloka 領導者留下一句話，您最想說什麼？
  • [EN] In one sentence, what is the core identity of Nagaloka that must never be changed, no matter how much the world transforms?
  • [TW] 用一句話來說，無論世界如何變化，Nagaloka 最核心、絕對不能被改變的本質是什麼？
