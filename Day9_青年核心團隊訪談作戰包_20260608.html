Day 9 青年世代核心團隊訪談作戰包 (Nagaloka)

時間：2026 年 6 月 8 日 受訪者：Bhanu (巴努) 與 Sangharsh (桑格什) 核心角色：龍世學園 (Nagaloka) 新一代核心營運團隊

這是一份針對接下龍世學園重擔的「青年世代」所擬定的訪談大綱。他們擁有頂尖學歷，扛著巨大的生存壓力與創辦人的期待，在現代社會的洪流中試圖守護並延續安貝卡博士的人間佛教願景。

💡 主題一：世代交替與方法論的轉變 (Generational Shift in Methods)

1. 從啟蒙到體制化作戰

[EN] Lokamitra and Vivekratna's generation relied heavily on building grassroots faith and conducting retreats. As the new core team equipped with advanced degrees (like your MSW from TISS), how do you approach Dr. Ambedkar's mission differently? What modern tools do you use that the pioneers didn't have? [TW] 世友居士和 Vivekratna 那一代，主要依靠建立基層信仰和透過閉關來啟蒙大眾。作為具備頂尖學歷（如 TISS 碩士）的新一代核心團隊，你們推進安貝卡博士使命的方式有何不同？你們使用了哪些開拓者當年沒有的現代工具？

2. 生存壓力與純粹奉獻的拉扯

[EN] Vivekratna mentioned yesterday that younger people today face immense financial pressure to support their families, making it difficult to commit to the "careerless" volunteer life that the founders lived. How do you and your peers balance modern survival and professional ambition with a deep commitment to this social movement? [TW] Vivekratna 昨天提到，現在的年輕人面臨巨大的養家財務壓力，很難像創辦人那樣過著「無生涯規劃」的純粹奉獻生活。您和您的同儕，是如何在現代生存壓力、專業野心，與對這場社會運動的深厚承諾之間取得平衡的？

💡 主題二：教育、階級與真實世界的衝擊 (Education & Grassroots Reality)

3. 菁英教育與草根現實的對接

[EN] You represent the new wave of highly educated Dalit youth. When you return from elite academic institutions to run Nagaloka, how do you bridge the gap between academic theories (like structural poverty) and the lived reality of the grassroots students coming from 26 different states? [TW] 你們代表了受過高等教育的達利特青年新浪潮。當你們從菁英學術機構回到龍世學園工作時，你們是如何在學術理論（如結構性貧窮）與來自 26 個邦的基層學生的真實生活困境之間，建立起溝通橋樑的？

4. 現代社會中的勞動尊嚴

[EN] At Nagaloka, everyone must participate in manual labor like cleaning the campus and toilets to practice absolute equality. In modern India, where many educated youths desire comfortable desk jobs, how do you guide your students to find dignity in manual labor and realize that it is a crucial part of spiritual liberation? [TW] 在龍世學園，所有人都必須參與打掃校園與廁所等日常勞動，以實踐絕對的平權精神。在現代印度，許多受過教育的年輕人都渴望舒適的辦公室工作，您們是如何引導學生在這些親力親為的勞動中看見尊嚴，並體會到這也是精神解放的一部分？

💡 主題三：營運現實與佛教慈悲的交鋒 (Administrative Reality & Buddhist Metta)

5. 財務自立與核心精神的平衡

[EN] As the core team handling Administration and Income Generation, you face the immense pressure of running this large campus. We know traditional donations are no longer enough. What innovative ways are you trying to make Nagaloka financially self-sustaining, and how do you ensure you don't lose the core Buddhist spirit in the process? [TW] 作為負責行政與創收的核心團隊，你們面臨著營運這個龐大校區的巨大壓力。我們知道傳統的捐款已經不夠了，你們正在嘗試哪些創新的方法來讓龍世學園達到財務自立？在這過程中，又如何確保不失去佛法的核心精神？

6. 科技時代的雙面刃

[EN] Vivekratna compared modern smartphones to a "drug" that distracts youth. Yet, your generation must leverage digital technology and social media for organization and outreach. How do you utilize these tools for your mission without losing the focus and mindfulness required for Dharma practice? [TW] Vivekratna 將現代智慧型手機比喻為讓年輕人分心的「毒品」。然而，你們這代人必須利用網路科技來進行推廣和組織。您如何利用這些工具推進使命，同時又不會讓學生失去佛法修行所需的專注與正念？

💡 主題四：世友居士的遺產與認可 (Lokamitra's Legacy & Validation)

7. 「被看見」的渴望與自我期許

[EN] In our casual chat, I noticed you asked if Lokamitra mentioned you in our interviews. It shows that even as you independently run this campus, the acknowledgment from the founder still means a lot. As you take on this heavy burden, what do you most want to prove to Lokamitra? What do you hope he sees in your generation? [TW] 在我們閒聊時，我注意到您順口問起「世友居士有沒有提到我？」。這讓我感受到，即使你們已經是獨當一面的管理核心，創辦人的「看見與認可」對你們依然意義重大。在承接這份重擔的過程中，你們最渴望向世友居士證明什麼？希望他能在你們身上看到什麼特質？

8. 與創辦人世代的意見相左

[EN] Every generation has disagreements with the one before it. Looking back at the decisions made by the pioneer generation (Lokamitra, Vivekratna), what is one strategic decision you deeply respect, and one approach that your younger team is actively trying to change? [TW] 每個世代都會與前輩有意見分歧。回顧開拓者世代（世友、Vivekratna）所做的決定，哪一項戰略決策是你們深感敬佩的？又有哪一種做法或思維，是你們年輕團隊現在正積極試圖改變的？

💡 主題五：未竟之業與未來願景 (Unfinished Business & The Future)

9. 校友網絡的真實挑戰

[EN] The "Alumni Network" now spans across India. From your perspective managing this on the ground, what is the most brutal or realistic challenge these alumni face when they go back to their home states, and how are you practically supporting them? [TW] 「校友網絡」現在遍布全印度。從你們這些在第一線管理網路的世代來看，這些校友回到家鄉後，面臨的最殘酷或最現實的挑戰是什麼？你們目前是如何實質地支援他們的？

10. 寫給三十年後的備忘錄

[EN] If you look 30 years ahead—when your generation has fully taken the helm and you are the senior elders—what is your ultimate vision for Dr. Ambedkar's "Prabuddha Bharat"? What is the one "unfinished business" of the pioneer generation that you are most determined to complete? [TW] 如果放眼三十年後——當你們這代人完全掌舵、成為資深長老時——您對安貝卡博士「覺悟的印度（Prabuddha Bharat）」的最終願景是什麼？世友居士那一代有哪一項「未竟之業」，是您下定決心一定要完成的？
