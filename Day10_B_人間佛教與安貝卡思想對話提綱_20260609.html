Day 10 B 人間佛教與安貝卡思想對話提綱 (Mumbai)

時間：2026 年 6 月 9 日 核心主題：印順導師的「人間佛教」與安貝卡博士的「社會解放佛教」之跨文化對話 對話背景： 世友居士（Lokamitra）是這座跨文化橋樑的親歷者。1989 年，他親自拜訪印順導師，並長期在印度實踐這兩個世界的對接。本提綱旨在引導世友居士深入闡述他如何將「西方三寶佛教會（Triratna）的精神修行」與「安貝卡的反種姓解放運動」相結合，並回應華人與印度佛教界之間的核心誤解。

💡 核心對話與提問設計 (Core Dialogue & Questions)

Q1. 頂住「正統性」的質疑：業報、輪迴與僧伽的重新排序

[EN] Chinese and Taiwanese Buddhists are deeply rooted in the traditional concepts of karma, rebirth, and the monastic Sangha. When they see Indian New Buddhists questioning rebirth (to reject the justification of caste oppression) and practicing without traditional monks, they often react with skepticism: "Is this even real Buddhism?" How did you navigate and defend the Dharma legitimacy of Nagaloka when communicating with the Taiwanese/Chinese Buddhist community?

[TW] 華人佛教界深植於「因果業報」、「輪迴」與「唯有出家比丘才是僧伽主體」的傳統。當他們看到印度新佛教徒因為拒絕以「前世業障」合理化種姓壓迫，因而對輪迴抱持質疑，且以「非僧非俗」的法行者（Dhammachari）為核心時，往往會產生「這是不是真正的佛教？」的懷疑。您在與台灣或華人佛教界溝通時，是如何回應這種「正統性」的質疑，並為 Nagaloka 的弘法與修持路線辯護的？

💡 延伸追問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Did you encounter donors or monastics in Taiwan who flatly refused to support Nagaloka because they felt the Indian movement was "too political" or "spiritually shallow"? How did you handle that?
  • [TW] 您是否曾遇到台灣的捐款人或法師，因為覺得印度的新佛教運動「太過政治化」或「缺乏靈性深度」而拒絕支持 Nagaloka？當時您是如何溝通的？
  • [EN] How do you explain to a traditional Buddhist that "giving up belief in past-life karma" is actually a necessary step for a Dalit to realize the core Buddhist teachings of Non-Self (Anatta) and human dignity?
  • [TW] 您會如何向傳統佛教徒解釋：對於一個達利特人來說，「放下對前世業報的迷信」，反而是他們體證佛法核心「無我（Anatta）」與恢復人類尊嚴的必要步驟？

Q2. 1989 年歷史性會面：拜會印順導師的微言大義

[EN] In 1989, facilitated by Prof. Yu Hsiang-Chou, you personally met Ven. Yin Shun. The Master spent his entire life fighting the degeneration of Chinese Buddhism into mere rituals, ghosts, and funeral services, advocating instead for "Humanistic Buddhism" centered on this-worldly human relief. In that historic meeting, when you described to him how Indian Dalits were using the Dharma for dignity and social liberation, how did he respond? Did he see in your work the actualization of his own vision?

[TW] 1989 年，在游祥洲教授的引介下，您親自拜會了印順導師。印順導師一生也在對抗漢傳佛教「過度儀式化、死後化、鬼神化」的流弊，極力提倡「以人為本」的人間佛教。在那次歷史性的會面中，當您向他描述印度達利特人如何透過佛法爭取尊嚴與社會解放時，導師給了您什麼樣的具體回應？他是否在您們的實踐中，看到了他自己思想在印度的回聲？

💡 延伸追問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Yin Shun wrote extensively about returning to early Buddhist scriptures (Agamas) and combining them with the Bodhisattva path. Did you feel a strong intellectual alignment with him, given that Triratna also emphasizes early Buddhism and the Bodhisattva ideal?
  • [TW] 印順導師的著作極力強調回歸早期經典（阿含經），並將其與大乘菩薩道結合。鑑於三寶佛教會（Triratna）同樣強調早期佛教與菩薩誓願，您當時是否感受到與導師在思想上有著極深的共鳴？
  • [EN] What was the most encouraging or memorable thing Master Yin Shun said to you during that meeting?
  • [TW] 在那次會面中，印順導師對您說過最令您鼓舞、或最讓您難忘的一句話是什麼？

Q3. 「社會運動」與「心靈修行」的雙向張力

[EN] Within the Indian movement, there is a constant tension: if you focus too much on social action, Buddhism risks devolving into a mere anti-caste NGO; yet, in the Western Triratna context, some might argue that heavy social work distracts from "pure meditation and spiritual evolution." How did you strike a dynamic balance between Ambedkar’s "social equality revolution" and Sangharakshita’s focus on "inner refuge and spiritual transformation"? Did you ever feel pulled by both sides?

[TW] 在印度新佛教運動內部，存在著一種雙向的張力：一不小心，佛教就可能退化為純粹的反種姓社會運動或 NGO；但在西方三寶僧團的脈絡中，可能又有人認為過度投入社會工作會妨礙「純粹的禪修與心靈演化」。您是如何在安貝卡的「社會平等革命」與僧護的「內在三寶皈依與心靈轉化」之間取得動態平衡的？您曾面臨過兩邊的拉扯嗎？

💡 延伸追問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Did Sangharakshita ever express concern that the Indian wing (TBMSG) was becoming too focused on social welfare projects rather than pure spiritual practice?
  • [TW] 僧護大師是否曾擔心過：印度的分支（TBMSG）花費了太多心力在社會福利項目上，而偏離了純粹的精神修持？
  • [EN] When running social action programs (like hostels or clinics), how did you ensure the staff and youth understood these actions not as "charity" but as the direct practice of the Dharma (Dhamma Practice)?
  • [TW] 在推動社會行動（如學生宿舍或醫療診所）時，您是如何確保工作人員和青年理解這些行動不只是「做慈善」，而是佛法修持（Dhamma Practice）的直接展現？

Q4. Dhammachari 制度：打破出家階序的精神革命

[EN] The Dhammachari/Dhammacharini order established by Triratna in India is a radical innovation of a "neither monk nor lay" committed system. It dismantled the Theravada monopoly where only robed monastics constitute the Sangha. How did you convince both the Indian public and overseas donors that these Dhammacharis—who do not wear traditional robes and may have families—possess the spiritual authority and depth of devotion to lead a Dharma community?

[TW] 三寶佛教會在印度推行的「法行者（Dhammachari/Dhammacharini）」制度，是一種「非僧非俗」的終身承諾體制。這打破了傳統南傳佛教「唯有剃度出家比丘才是僧伽主體」的壟斷，也不同於漢傳的傳統。您是如何說服印度大眾與海外捐助者：這些沒有穿著傳統僧袍、甚至有家庭的法行者，同樣擁有帶領精神社群的宗教神聖性與無私奉獻的深度？

💡 延伸追問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] In the early days, did traditional Theravada monks in India try to undermine your work by telling the new Buddhists that your Dhammacharis were "fake monks" or that their ordinations were invalid? How did you respond?
  • [TW] 在早期，印度的傳統南傳比丘是否曾試圖排擠您們的工作，告訴新皈依的佛教徒說您們的法行者是「假和尚」、或者您們的授戒是無效的？您當時如何應對？
  • [EN] Ambedkar’s 22 vows explicitly reject Hindu rituals. Do you see the Dhammachari ordination as the practical container that allows new Buddhists to live out those 22 vows in a deeply spiritual way, rather than just a political declaration?
  • [TW] 安貝卡的 22 條誓願明確拒絕了印度教儀式。您是否認為，法行者授戒是一個實質的精神容器，讓新佛教徒能以一種深具靈性的方式活出這 22 條誓願，而不僅僅是停留在政治宣告？

Q5. 慈心（Metta）能否治癒種姓創傷？

[EN] Dalits carry centuries of historical trauma and micro-violence, which naturally breeds deep anger. While Buddhism emphasizes Metta (loving-kindness) and letting go of hatred, the Ambedkarite movement is inherently revolutionary and confrontational. In Nagaloka's training, how do you guide young students to transform their righteous anger against unjust systems into Dharma-based wisdom and compassion, rather than allowing Buddhism to be used as a political tool for venting hatred against upper castes?

[TW] 達利特人承受著千百年的種姓壓迫與微觀暴力，心中累積了深沉的憤怒與創傷。佛教強調「慈心（Metta）」與放下瞋恨，但安貝卡運動本身具有強烈的革命性與對抗性。在 Nagaloka 的訓練中，您們是如何引導年輕學生將「對不義制度的正義憤怒」，轉化為佛法中的「慈悲與智慧」，而不讓佛教變成另一種宣洩對上層種姓仇恨的政治工具？

💡 延伸追問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Have you had students who struggled to practice meditation on Metta Bhavana (development of loving-kindness) because their hearts were filled with too much trauma or rage against upper castes? How did you guide them?
  • [TW] 您是否曾遇到學生在修習「慈心觀（Metta Bhavana）」時感到極度困難，因為他們心中對上層種姓有著太多的創傷與憤怒？您們是如何引導他們的？
  • [EN] Ambedkar famously said, "Fraternity is only another name for democracy." How does the Triratna concept of spiritual friendship (Kalyana Mitrata) turn Ambedkar's political concept of "fraternity" into a lived, healing reality?
  • [TW] 安貝卡曾說：「友愛（Fraternity）只是民主的另一個名字。」三寶佛教會提倡的「善知識／精神友誼（Kalyana Mitrata）」概念，是如何將安貝卡筆下這個政治學的「友愛」概念，轉化為日常生活中具備療癒力的真實體驗的？
