一、贍部洲（Jambudvipa）Trust 與「曼努斯基」（Day4 訪談後補核）

1.1 核心句

Day4 已由彌勒種完成第一次贍部洲／「曼努斯基」／龍樹學院組織史訪談。今日不重問整套敘事，只補法律文件、年份、照片、可公開案例與 Kondhanpur / MWRC 的空間脈絡。

1.2 必問：正式名稱與時序

  • "Can we see or photograph the official registration / brochure for Jambudvipa and Manuski?"
  • "Is the legal name simply Jambudvipa, or should the book say Jambudvipa Trust?"
  • "Can you confirm the 1999 / 2007 / 2016 dates from the Day4 Maitreyanath interview?"
  • "What was the first office or working space? Was it Prashant Apartment?"
  • "Who were the first trustees, staff, or core assistants, and which names can be printed?"
  • "Which parts of the Manuski atrocity / human-rights work can be publicly mentioned?"
  • "What is the correct way to describe Manuski in the book: project, centre, network, fellowship, foundation, or something else?"

1.3 必問：贍部洲的定位

  • "Jambudvipa is a name from Buddhist texts for the Indian subcontinent. How did Lokamitra understand that name?"
  • "Was it mainly about Dharma practice, Buddhist education, seminars, or the relationship between Dharma and society?"
  • "How was it connected with Nagaloka from the beginning?"
  • "What could be done through Jambudvipa that could not be done while Lokamitra was formally president of the earlier centres?"

1.4 必問：「曼努斯基」的定位

  • "What does 'Manuski' mean in Marathi usage, and how is it connected with Dr. Ambedkar?"
  • "What were the first 25 Dalit-based NGOs or activists like? What made the network new?"
  • "What kinds of training did Manuski actually provide: capacity building, women's leadership, legal awareness, social action, fellowship training?"
  • "What did Manuski do in atrocity cases or urgent social situations? Which examples can be publicly mentioned?"
  • "How do you avoid making Manuski sound like charity from outside, rather than Dalit leadership building itself?"

1.5 材料索取清單

  • 贍部洲 Trust／「曼努斯基」官方名稱、註冊年份、簡介或章程。
  • 一頁式組織年表：大精舍 → 贍部洲 → 「曼努斯基」 → 龍樹學院／alumni programs。
  • 年報、簡報、brochure、課程表、training manual、Manuski Fellowship 文件。
  • 早期小公寓／第一個辦公室／現在 Manuski Centre 的照片或可公開描述。
  • 「曼努斯基」Network 初期 25 個 NGO／activists 的名單或類型說明。
  • 可公開提及的案例清單；哪些不可公開、不可寫入中文書稿。

1.6 寫作判斷

贍部洲要寫成「退到小處後反而看見大局」：從正式中心會長退下，轉入較低調、較自由、較能連接學者、社運者、達利特與部落組織的空間。「曼努斯基」是其中更靠近社會行動的一支。不要寫成 NGO 成功學；要寫成佛法、安貝卡思想、城市社群與本地領導力訓練交會的平台。

  • 與 CH09 龍樹學院敘事的地理／時間連結。

2.2 CH11 2001 事件（只在對方願意時問，不逼創傷）

紅線：事件可陳述事實，情緒由當事人自己給詞；不渲染暴力、不替 Vishaka 總結心境。決定不提告的是 Vishaka（非 Lokamitra 與她共同）。

  • 〔願意才問・對 Vishaka〕當時決定不提告，背後的考量與過程是什麼？〔EN: What were the considerations behind the decision not to press charges?〕
  • 〔對 Lokamitra〕這起事件在書中應如何定位，才不會把它寫成英雄或受難敘事？哪些細節可公開、哪些只能留白？
  • 母親婚禮禮金的故事（手稿線索）是否可寫、由誰來講最適合？

2.3 寫作判斷

Kondhanpur 是 CH11「二十五邦的種子」中少數的暴力事件。依政治審慎與反漫畫化原則：陳述有記錄的事實、不放大、不連結為「對 Lokamitra 的壓迫」；把敘事主權交回 Vishaka 自己手裡。
