二、現場觀察清單

A.【上午】Jivak 耆婆健康項目 + 達波迪貧民窟 — CH02／CH06／CH10

  • Jivak 健康點怎麼運作：誰看診、服務誰、規模、經費；早期那個 120 sq ft 鐵皮棚還在嗎？（CH06 事實：非 ten sq ft）
  • 五感落地：氣味、聲音、光線、地面、溫度——只記真實
  • ⚠️ 反貧窮戲劇化（CH02 紅線）：作者說「I rarely went into homes that poor」「dramatizing poverty…will end up making me a saint」。不要把極端貧窮寫成 Lokamitra 的日常視野；不渲染、不引發憐憫（CH04「beware of pity」）。
  • ⚠️ 去中產想像：勿用「living rooms / empty rooms」式投射（CH07：「they were full」）。
  • 人物立體：苦難之外，也記下幽默、日常、尊嚴（§1-G 反漫畫化）。

B.【下午】女生宿舍（Girls Hostel）— CH07（敏感：婚姻前景）

  • 宿舍運作：作息、人數、伙食、誰管理、經費
  • 學生背景：來自哪些村／社群、為何來、家庭狀況
  • ⚠️ 婚姻前景（CH07 事實）：村莊家庭不願送女兒，原因之一是「marriage prospects could be compromised」。用開放、尊重的問法，別預設、別獵奇。
  • 教育如何改變這些女孩的人生路徑（升學、就業、婚配）

C. 通則：拍照／錄音前徵得同意；隨手記待查名詞→晚上回填。

三、逐章提問

CH02（扎根）— 貧民窟、早期工作

A 對話與示範：我原本把貧窮寫得太戲劇化，您說「dramatizing…will make me a saint, which is quite inappropriate」、又說「I rarely went into homes that poor」→ 我已收斂。

  • 開口示範："You warned me that dramatizing poverty would turn you into a saint, which isn't right — and that you rarely went into the very poorest homes. I've toned all that down. Could you tell me what your actual contact with these communities was like?" B 逐章修改提問（開放）：
  1. "What was your actual, day-to-day relationship with the slum communities back then — where did you usually go, and where did you not?"（釐清他真實的接觸範圍，而非聖人化）
  2. "You once said you were 'certainly one of them as a Buddhist' — eating, staying, relaxing together. Can you tell me about one such ordinary day?"（CH02 原話，引出真實日常） C 現場場景提問：今天在貧民窟看到的，哪些是 1978–80 年代就有的、哪些是後來才變的？
  • "Of what I'm seeing in the slum today, what was already here in the late 1970s–80s, and what has changed since?"

CH06／CH10（Jivak 健康項目）

B 逐章修改提問（開放）：

  1. "How did the Jivak health work actually begin — whose idea, and what did it look like at the very start?"（勇賢 & 蓮慧的角色）
  2. "That early tin-shed clinic — what actually happened there day to day?"（CH06：120 sq ft，請他還原真實場景，非想像）

CH07（女生宿舍）

B 逐章修改提問（開放）：

  1. "Why were village families hesitant to send their daughters — and how did that change once they did?"（婚姻前景，敏感、開放）
  2. "What does an education here actually change for a girl — in her own family, in her future?"

四、達善晚餐：補他的生命史（🟢 關係＋個人線）

餐桌氣氛輕鬆，最適合問他自己（不是工作）。開放、好奇，不查戶口。

  • "Were you born into a Buddhist family, or did you convert? What brought you to it?"
  • "You spent 17 years in Britain — what were you doing there, and what made you come back?"
  • "That image of the thousand-armed, thousand-eyed Avalokiteshvara you mentioned — where did you first learn it?"（接 Manuski 筆記 §2.2）
