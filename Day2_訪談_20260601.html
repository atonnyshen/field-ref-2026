Day 2（6/1）訪談作戰包

今天的策略改變 Day 1 沒有照原作戰包逐題進行，實際變成 Lokamitra 帶著你看照片、講早期社會工作與空間變遷。這不是偏離，而是更好的方法：照片讓他自己帶出記憶，降低誘導，也避開他最在意的「被代寫內心」問題。
Day 2 的任務不是把 Day 1 沒問的 CH01 題硬補回來，而是把昨天照片裡的線索拿到 Jeevak、Dapodi 貧民區與女生宿舍現場核實。

〇、今日優先順序

優先 任務 原因
1 現場核實 Jeevak / 耆婆健康教育中心 Day1 照片提到 Centre for Health and Educational Work、doctor/nurse、pathology、1993 logo，明天正好到現場；SSOT 已統一為 Jeevak Health Education Centre
2 觀察 Dapodi 貧民區「原地改善」 Lokamitra 說二十年前是 rough brick / tin sheets / plastic，現在是 proper but basic buildings
3 區分「有效介入」與 development work Lokamitra 明確說他們沒有技能與能力做完整 development work，只能做幼兒園、study class、文化活動、醫療等
4 女生宿舍：教育如何改變人生路徑 原 Day2 重點仍有效，但要接上 Day1 的「教育 → 穩定工作／尊嚴」線
5 晚上若見 Lokamitra：延續照片法 + scaffold 他已明確說想先看全書 chapter-by-chapter scaffold，不需要整本正文

  • 下午 15:30：女生宿舍（Girls Hostel）

  • 晚上：達善請吃飯，適合補達善個人生命史與早期組織記憶

⚠️ 紅線沿用 Day1：不使用 war / battle / fight / burden；不把貧困戲劇化；不把 Lokamitra 寫成聖人；不預設種姓、婚姻、貧困者的內心。

三、上午 A：Jeevak / 耆婆健康教育中心

3.1 現場必看

  • 招牌、牌匾、正式英文／馬拉地文名稱；優先核對是否為 Jeevak Health Education Centre。
  • 是否仍稱為 Centre for Health and Educational Work。
  • 目前空間配置：診間、教室、辦公室、等候區、藥品／檢驗空間、兒童活動空間。
  • 是否仍有醫療工作；若已停止，何時停止、為什麼停止？
  • 是否有早期照片、logo、年報、病理檢驗或營養檢查紀錄。
  • 是否能看到與 1993 logo 相關的實物或照片。
  • 現場誰能講早期故事：醫生、護士、管理者、舊社工、學生、居民。

3.2 開口問法（英文）

  1. "What is the official name of this centre, and how should I spell it?"
    先把 Jeevak 的正式名稱與馬拉地文名稱解決；Jivak / Jivaka 只作舊筆記或 ASR 待核拼法。

  2. "Lokamitra mentioned health and educational work here. What exactly was done in the early days, and what is still being done now?"

  3. "Was there a clinic, a pathology lab, nutrition work, Balwadi classes, study classes, or all of these at different times?"

  4. "What role did Virabhadra and Padmasuri actually play at the beginning?"

  5. "Lokamitra mentioned someone I heard as Shakinan. In our notes this may correspond to Shakyanand, the retired laboratory technician who later wore the yellow robe. Do you know who that was, and whether he did pathology or laboratory work here?"

  6. "Are there any old photographs, annual reports, signs, or documents that show what this centre looked like in the 1980s or 1990s?"

3.3 寫作判斷

🟠 Jeevak 不能只寫成「醫療慈善」。它應該被放在 Day1 得到的脈絡裡：佛法變成社區基礎設施。幼兒園、study class、Balwadi、醫療、病理／檢驗、營養、健康教育，都是讓人能站起來的條件。

四、上午 B：Dapodi 貧民區／周邊社區

4.1 現場必看

  • 建材：是否能看出 rough brick、tin sheets、plastic 到 proper basic buildings 的轉變？
  • 巷道寬度、排水、雨季水痕、橋下或低窪處。
  • 房屋是自建、加高、政府改建、開發商改建，還是混合？
  • local relationships：小店、工坊、學校、佛堂、社區中心、診所、補習班、婦女活動。
  • 是否仍有新移入人口；誰搬走、誰留下、誰往上流動？
  • 大精舍／Jeevak／社區中心與居民日常的距離：走路多久？誰會去？
  • 是否能找到照片中的 dirty water / hell / golf course 對照：水流、排水、牆、道路、高價土地與貧民區的相鄰關係。

4.2 開口問法（英文）

  1. "Lokamitra said that twenty years ago many houses here were rough brick, tin sheets and plastic. What has changed since then?"

  2. "Did people improve their houses where they already lived, or were they moved and rebuilt elsewhere?"

  3. "What amenities changed first: water, drainage, electricity, roads, school access, health work?"

  4. "What kinds of local relationships or small businesses make it important for people to remain here?"

  5. "When you worked here, what could you actually do effectively — education, health, Balwadi, study classes, cultural activities?"

  6. "What could you not do? What required government, town planning, or other skills?"

4.3 不要這樣問

避免問法 改成
"How did you develop the slum?" "Where could you make an effective intervention?"
"Did you change these people's lives?" "What changed for children or families who joined the classes?"
"Was it terrible here?" "What were the actual conditions then, and what is different now?"
"Were people helpless?" "What were people already doing to improve their own situation?"

🟠 寫作判斷：Day1 最重要的概念是 patchwork。早期社會工作不是翻新整個城市，而是在具體破洞處補上一塊：一間幼兒園、一堂 study class、一場營養檢查、一個可聚集的中心。

五、下午：女生宿舍（Girls Hostel）

5.1 現場必看

  • 宿舍人數、年齡、年級、來源村莊或社群。
  • 房間、床位、書桌、廚房、餐食、浴室、讀書空間。
  • 管理者是誰？與學生關係如何？一天作息如何？
  • 經費來源：trust、donor、家庭部分負擔、校友支持？
  • 學生的未來想像：升學、工作、回鄉、城市就業、公職、婚姻。
  • 是否有校友回來支持宿舍。

5.2 敏感題：婚姻前景

原 CH07 提到：村莊家庭不願送女兒，原因之一是 marriage prospects could be compromised。這題要問，但不能獵奇。

安全問法：

  1. "When families first send daughters here, what are they most worried about?"
  2. "Have those worries changed over the years?"
  3. "How does education here affect a girl's choices later — study, work, family, marriage?"
  4. "Do former students stay connected with the hostel?"

5.3 寫作判斷

🟠 女生宿舍不能寫成「被拯救的女孩」。要寫成教育如何改變選項：她們能讀書、住得安全、有同儕、有管理者、有未來想像，也要面對家庭、村莊、婚姻與經濟現實。尊嚴不等於漂亮敘事，尊嚴是具體條件。

六、晚上：達善晚餐

晚餐不適合查問式逼問，適合讓達善說自己。目標是補他作為「大精舍制度產品」與後來執行者的生命線。

6.1 輕鬆開口

  • "Were you born into a Buddhist family, or did you come to Buddhism later?"
  • "What first brought you into this movement?"
  • "You said you were trained at Mahavihara. What did that training do to you personally?"
  • "You spent many years in Britain. What did you do there, and what made you return?"
  • "When you look at the work now, what has become stronger, and what has become harder?"

6.2 可追問 Day1 照片線索

  • 釋迦難陀 (Shakyanand) / Shakinan / retired laboratory or pathology worker 是否認識？
  • Pimpri 1983 community centre 是否有更多記憶？
  • 早期 BDD chawls / corridor programs 是否他也參與？
  • Jeevak 的醫療工作現在為何縮小或改變？

七、若 17:00 見 Lokamitra

7.1 不硬補 CH01，先承接昨天

開場可說：

"Yesterday's photo conversation was very helpful. It gave me a much better sense of the whole world behind the book. If you are happy, I would like to continue working from photographs or places, because it lets the memories come in your own order."

7.2 Scaffold 問法

  1. "You said it would be better if I sent you a chapter-by-chapter scaffold rather than all the writing. Would one page per chapter be too much, or useful?"
  2. "What would you want to see in each chapter: main events, people, themes, or the questions I still need to check?"
  3. "Would you prefer that scaffold in English first?"
  4. "Are there any words in English that you want me to avoid throughout the whole book?"

7.3 英文參照稿提案（若氣氛適合才提）

"Because of your concern about AI translation, I am thinking of preparing an English reference text for your review as we work on the Chinese book. It would not be a published English edition at this stage. It would simply help you check the wording, facts, and any political or cultural sensitivities. If one day you feel an English edition should be published, we would be happy for you to arrange that through your own publisher. Our publisher in Taiwan would only ask for acknowledgement, not payment."

7.4 如果他主動談 CH01，才問

  • "When you first came to India, what did Pune mean to you then: Buddhism, yoga, people, or a mixture of all three?"
  • "Did your experience of creating Buddhist spaces in England help you when you began working in India?"
