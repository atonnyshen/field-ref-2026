Day 9 B 人間佛教與安貝卡思想對話提綱 (Mumbai)

時間：2026 年 6 月 8 日 核心主題：印順導師的「人間佛教」與安貝卡博士的「社會解放佛教」之跨文化對話 對話背景： 這份提綱專門為您與世友居士（Lokamitra）對談設計。考慮到口說與現場訪談，所有的英文提問已改寫為非常簡單、直白、口語化的短句。您可以很輕鬆地唸出來，不用擔心生硬的學術詞彙。

💡 核心對話與提問設計 (Core Dialogue & Questions)

Q1. 業障、輪迴與僧伽的疑慮 (Questions on Karma, Rebirth, and Monks)

[EN] In Taiwan and China, Buddhists believe in karma, rebirth, and traditional monks. But in Nagaloka, you do not focus on rebirth, and you do not have traditional monks. Some Chinese Buddhists might ask: "Is this real Buddhism?" How do you explain our way of Buddhism to them?

[TW] 在台灣和中國，佛教徒非常相信因果、輪迴和傳統的出家僧人。但在 Nagaloka，您們不強調輪迴，也沒有傳統的出家僧人。有些華人佛教徒可能會問：「這是真正的佛教嗎？」您是如何向他們解釋我們這種佛教路線的？

💡 延伸追問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Did any Taiwanese donors refuse to help because they thought Nagaloka was "too political" or "not spiritual enough"?
  • [TW] 曾有台灣捐款人因為覺得 Nagaloka「太政治化」或「不夠修行」而拒絕幫忙嗎？
  • [EN] How do you explain to them that dropping the idea of "past-life karma" actually helps Dalits gain dignity?
  • [TW] 您如何向他們解釋：放下「前世業障」的想法，反而能幫助達利特人找到尊嚴？

Q2. 拜會印順導師 (Meeting Ven. Yin Shun in 1989)

[EN] In 1989, you met Ven. Yin Shun in Taiwan. He also wanted to change Chinese Buddhism—making it focus on this life, not just rituals or death. When you told him about the Dalit movement and Buddhism in India, what did he say? Did he encourage you?

[TW] 1989 年，您在台灣拜訪了印順導師。他當時也想改革華人佛教，讓佛教關注此生，而不是只做儀式或面對死亡。當您向他報告印度達利特運動與佛教發展時，他對您說了什麼？他有鼓勵您嗎？

💡 延伸追問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] What is the most memorable thing Master Yin Shun said to you?
  • [TW] 印順導師對您說過最難忘的一句話是什麼？
  • [EN] Did you feel that you and Yin Shun had the same dream for Buddhism?
  • [TW] 您當時是否覺得，您和印順導師對佛教有著相同的夢想？

Q3. 「社會運動」與「禪修修行」的平衡 (Balancing Social Action and Meditation)

[EN] Some people might think Nagaloka is just an NGO for social work, not a temple. Others might think social work distracts you from pure meditation. How do you balance social reform with personal spiritual practice? Did you ever feel stuck between the two?

[TW] 有些人可能會覺得 Nagaloka 只是一個做社會工作的 NGO，而不是寺院。但也有些人覺得做社會工作會讓人無法專心禪修。您是如何平衡「社會改革」與「個人修行」的？您曾覺得被這兩邊拉扯嗎？

💡 延伸追問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Did Sangharakshita ever worry that Nagaloka was doing too much social work and not enough Dharma practice?
  • [TW] 僧護大師是否曾擔心過：Nagaloka 做了太多社會工作，而偏離了佛法修行？
  • [EN] How do you make sure the young students see running hostels or clinics as part of their Dharma practice, not just charity?
  • [TW] 您如何確保年輕學生知道，管理宿舍或診所是他們佛法修行的一部分，而不只是做慈善？

Q4. Dhammachari 制度：非僧非俗 (The Dhammachari System: Neither Monks nor Laypeople)

[EN] In India, Triratna uses the "Dhammachari" system. They are not traditional monks, and they can have families. But they commit to the Dharma for life. How do you convince Indian people and overseas donors that Dhammacharis have the spiritual authority to lead?

[TW] 在印度，您們採用「法行者（Dhammachari）」制度。他們不是傳統的出家僧人，可以有家庭，但終身承諾奉獻給佛法。您是如何說服印度大眾和海外捐款人，法行者同樣有資格帶領大眾修行？

💡 延伸追問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Did traditional monks in India ever call your Dhammacharis "fake monks" or say their ordination was not real? How did you respond?
  • [TW] 印度傳統的出家僧人曾說您們的法行者是「假僧人」或授戒是假的嗎？您當時怎麼回應？
  • [EN] Ambedkar's 22 vows reject Hindu rituals. Do you feel the Dhammachari system helps new Buddhists live out these vows spiritually?
  • [TW] 安貝卡的 22 條誓願拒絕了印度教儀式。您覺得法行者制度有幫助新佛教徒在精神上活出這些誓願嗎？

Q5. 慈心與創傷憤怒 (Can Loving-kindness (Metta) Heal Anger?)

[EN] Dalits have suffered caste discrimination for centuries, which brings a lot of anger. Buddhism teaches Metta (loving-kindness) and letting go of anger. How do you teach Metta to students who carry so much trauma and anger? How do you prevent Buddhism from becoming just a tool for political revenge?

[TW] 達利特人承受了幾個世紀的種姓歧視，這帶來了很多憤怒。佛教教導慈心與放下憤怒。您如何向這些帶著創傷和憤怒的學生教授慈心？您如何避免佛教淪為純粹政治報復的工具？

💡 延伸追問 / Follow-up Questions
  • [EN] Did any students find it hard to practice Metta (loving-kindness) because they hated the upper castes too much? How did you guide them?
  • [TW] 曾有學生因為太恨上層種姓，而很難修習慈心觀嗎？您們怎麼引導他們？
  • [EN] Ambedkar said "fraternity" (brotherhood) is key to democracy. How does our practice of spiritual friendship (Kalyana Mitrata) make this "fraternity" real in daily life?
  • [TW] 安貝卡曾說「友愛」是民主的關鍵。我們對「精神友誼」的修持，是如何在日常生活中讓「友愛」成真的？
