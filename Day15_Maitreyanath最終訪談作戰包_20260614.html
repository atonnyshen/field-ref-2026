深度訪談大綱：彌勒種 (Maitreyanath) 最終回訪談

這是最後一次與彌勒種（Maitreyanath）的深度訪談。目標不是再問一輪組織介紹，而是把書中仍缺的「人、場景、代價、判斷、邊界」補起來。先前 Day7 已取得許多關鍵內容：23 歲離開政府工作、第一任男生宿舍舍監、女生宿舍家長會策略、WSP 1997-2007 轉型、NTI / Nagaloka / Jambudvipa / TBMSG / Bahujan Hitay 的組織邊界。這些內容不再原樣重問。

本次提問的核心是：讓他從「官方回答」轉入「親歷者回答」。問題要引出具體年份、人物、房間、夜晚、猶豫、失敗、身體感、家庭代價與公開邊界。

已答內容：不要原樣重問

主題 已有答案 Day15 處理
社會工作即修行 Day7 已答：從安貝卡熱情開始，後來透過 Lokamitra 學佛法，轉成 metta bhavana、慷慨與仁慈的修行。 不再問定義；改問具體場景、失敗與代價。
男生宿舍 Day7 已答：23 歲離職後成為第一任舍監，照顧 42 名 10-17 歲男孩。 只追問年份、地點、職銜、可公開案例。
女生宿舍 Day7 已答：早期五年困難，透過每年兩次家長會把恐懼轉成驕傲。 不再問策略；改問完整生命路徑與可公開匿名案例。
WSP Day7 已答：1997-2007 一般社區工作，2007 後轉向年輕女孩與家暴。 只追問具體人名、地點、九個貧民窟名稱與公開邊界。

現場使用提醒

  • 先把「我們不是要重問已回答的內容」說清楚，讓他知道這次是在補書稿最後缺口。
  • 英文句子保持短、清楚、能口說。必要時先說中文意圖，再問英文。
  • 敏感個案先問：「Can this be public? Should we anonymize it?」
  • 如果他開始給官方答案，追問：「Do you remember one concrete scene? Where were you? Who was there?」

Q1. 請校正您的職掌年表 (Your Timeline and Responsibilities)

中文提問概念：我們目前從不同來源拼出您的角色：23 歲離開政府工作、第一任男生宿舍舍監、1996 年到 Nagpur、2001 年回 Pune 支持世友、後來在 Nagpur 負責 hostels、Balwadis、Arts and Sports Center，也曾被 Vivekratna 稱為 executive director。請您把這份年表校正成書中可以使用的版本。

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "I want to make your role accurate in the book."
  • "We have several dates and titles, but I do not want to guess."
  • "Could you correct your timeline for me?"
  • "When did you leave your government job, and what was your first formal role?"
  • "When did you move to Nagpur, and what responsibilities did you take there?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 您當時的正式職銜是 warden、social worker、executive director，還是其他名稱？ 💬 "What was your official title at each stage?"

  • Vivekratna 說您負責 social projects，他負責 Dhamma side。這個分工準確嗎？ 💬 "Vivekratna said you managed the social projects while he focused on the Dhamma side. Is that accurate?"

  • 您的中文名「道明」與「彌勒種」應如何在書中使用？ 💬 "You also have the Chinese name Daoming. How should we use Daoming and Maitreyanath in the book?"

Q2. Pardi 女性案例：時間、地點與正確框架 (The Pardi Woman Case)

中文提問概念：世友提到一位帕爾迪族（Pardi）女性，參加了 2010 年龍樹學院「消滅種姓——下一步」工作坊，後來對抗部落 panchayat。世友印象中她來自 Maharashtra，不是 Gujarat。這個案例過去被標為「different situation」，不能簡化成一般種姓故事。請您核對細節與框架。

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "Lokamitra mentioned a Pardi woman."
  • "She attended the 2010 Annihilation of Caste workshop at Nagaloka."
  • "Later, she stood up to her tribe's panchayat."
  • "His memory is that she was from Maharashtra, not Gujarat."
  • "Do you remember the exact year she first contacted Manuski?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 她來自 Maharashtra 還是 Gujarat？若兩者都不準確，正確地點是哪裡？ 💬 "Was she from Maharashtra or Gujarat? Or was it another place?"

  • 她第一次聯絡 Manuski 是在 2010 年之前、當年，還是之後？ 💬 "Did she first contact Manuski before 2010, in 2010, or after that?"

  • 工作坊之後，她是否仍與 Manuski 或 Nagaloka 保持聯繫？ 💬 "After the workshop, did she stay involved with Manuski or Nagaloka?"

  • 這個故事應該用什麼框架寫？不是一般 caste atrocity，那它真正說明什麼？ 💬 "Lokamitra said this was a different situation. What is the right frame for this story?"

Q3. 第一個真正改變您的孩子或夜晚 (The First Child or Night That Changed You)

中文提問概念：Day7 已經談過您是第一任男生宿舍舍監，照顧 42 名孩子。這次不問制度，問一個可以寫成場景的生命轉折：哪一個孩子、哪一個夜晚、哪一次生病、憤怒或恐懼，讓您真正意識到自己不只是管理者，而是在被這份工作改造？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "You told me you were the first boys' hostel warden."
  • "You lived with 42 boys, like a father."
  • "Do you remember one child, one night, or one crisis that changed you?"
  • "A moment when you felt, 'This work is changing me.'"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 那個場景在哪裡？房間、床、聲音、氣味，您還記得什麼？ 💬 "Where were you? What do you still remember from that room or that night?"

  • 那時您做錯過什麼嗎？後來如何學會更好的方式？ 💬 "Did you make any mistake then? What did you learn from it?"

  • 這個案例可否匿名寫入書中？ 💬 "Can this story be used in the book if we remove the name?"

Q4. 什麼時候慈悲最難？(When Metta Was Most Difficult)

中文提問概念：他容易給出「慈悲、非暴力、修行」的官方答案。這題要問最難的時刻：面對家暴、暴行、誣告、受害者創傷時，什麼時候最難保持慈心？有沒有一次您內心很憤怒、很疲憊，卻仍要做出不傷害人的決定？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "We often speak about Metta and non-violence."
  • "But in real cases, it must be very difficult."
  • "Do you remember a time when keeping Metta was hard?"
  • "Maybe in a domestic violence case, an atrocity case, or a false case against an activist."

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 您當時想保護誰？又怕傷害誰？ 💬 "Who were you trying to protect? Who were you afraid of hurting?"

  • 什麼時候會選擇社區介入？什麼時候必須報警或進入法院？ 💬 "When do you use community intervention, and when must you go to police or court?"

  • 您是否曾因為一個決定而整晚睡不著？ 💬 "Was there a decision that kept you awake at night?"

Q5. 您曾經如何校正 Lokamitra？(How You Corrected Lokamitra)

中文提問概念：避免把世友寫成永遠正確的外國導師。彌勒種是 close circle，不只是執行者。請他回憶：有沒有一次他必須對世友說「這樣不行」「這樣印度人不會接受」「這樣太快或太西方」？這能讓合作關係更真實。

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "You were not only helping Lokamitra."
  • "You were also correcting him when needed."
  • "Do you remember a time when you had to tell him, 'This will not work here'?"
  • "Or, 'This is too fast, too Western, or too risky'?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 他當時如何反應？防衛、沉默、接受，還是爭論？ 💬 "How did he react? Did he argue, listen, or change his mind?"

  • 您如何在尊敬他與保護在地現實之間取得平衡？ 💬 "How did you balance respect for him with protecting the local reality?"

  • 這件事最後有改變某個專案或決策嗎？ 💬 "Did that conversation change a project or a decision?"

Q6. Close Circle 的真實工作法 (How the Close Circle Really Worked)

中文提問概念：Day4 提到世友有大構想時，會先和 Vivekratna、彌勒種等 close circle 討論，再送 board。這題要取得「組織如何真的運作」的非官方肌理：咖啡、午餐、手寫信、每天兩三小時討論、意見分歧、誰最後拍板。

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "In the early years, how did the close circle really work?"
  • "I mean the daily meetings, coffee talks, lunch talks, and hard discussions."
  • "Can you describe one important decision that was shaped in this circle?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 什麼議題最常引起長時間討論：錢、人事、課程、政治風險，還是佛法方向？ 💬 "What issues created the longest discussions: money, people, courses, political risk, or Dharma direction?"

  • 如果世友、您、Vivekratna 不同意，通常誰會讓步？為什麼？ 💬 "When you, Lokamitra, and Vivekratna disagreed, who usually moved? Why?"

  • 有沒有一個決定，現在回頭看仍覺得「幸好當時那樣做」？ 💬 "Is there one decision you now feel grateful you made in that way?"

Q7. 四十年工作真正拿走了什麼？(What the Work Took From You)

中文提問概念：他已談工作使人成長；這次反過來問代價。四十多年社會工作，除了信念與成就，也必定拿走了家庭時間、健康、安定、個人願望或年輕時的可能人生。這題要觸動生命歷程，不要讓他只談組織成果。

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "You have given more than forty years to this work."
  • "We often talk about what the work gave you."
  • "But what did the work take from you?"
  • "Family time, health, money, private dreams, or another life you could have lived?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 您的家人最難接受的是什麼？ 💬 "What was hardest for your family to accept?"

  • 您有沒有後悔或懷疑過？不是理論上的，而是真實的一天。 💬 "Did you ever regret or doubt this path, even for one day?"

  • 如果年輕的您現在坐在面前，您會提醒他什麼？ 💬 "If your younger self were sitting here, what warning would you give him?"

Q8. 青年世代不能再照舊奉獻時 (When the Young Cannot Sacrifice in the Same Way)

中文提問概念：Day9 已問青年壓力。這次問彌勒種作為前輩的理解：如果年輕人不能像他 23 歲那樣放下一切，這是退步、現實，還是運動必須成熟的信號？怎樣讓下一代有薪資、家庭、專業尊嚴，同時不把 Nagaloka 變成普通機構？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "The younger team faces family pressure and financial pressure."
  • "They cannot always sacrifice in the same way your generation did."
  • "How do you understand this?"
  • "Is it a weakness, a reality, or a sign that the movement must mature?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 老一代對年輕人最大的誤解是什麼？ 💬 "What does the older generation misunderstand about the younger team?"

  • 年輕一代對創辦人世代最大的誤解又是什麼？ 💬 "What does the younger generation misunderstand about the founders?"

  • 怎樣的薪資與制度，既尊重他們的生活，又不讓工作只變成 job？ 💬 "What kind of salary and structure can support them without making this only a job?"

Q9. 一位女性如何從受助者變成領導者 (From Beneficiary to Leader)

中文提問概念：Day7 已有女生宿舍策略與 WSP 轉型；現在要找一條完整生命路徑。請他指出一位可公開或可匿名的人：曾受宿舍、WSP、Manuski 或教育系統幫助，後來成為工作者、領導者、法行者、老師，甚至改變機構做法。

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "We already understand the strategy of the girls' hostel and WSP."
  • "Now I need one life story."
  • "Can you think of one woman who moved from being helped to becoming a leader?"
  • "Someone who later changed the work itself."

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 她原本面臨什麼風險：失學、早婚、家暴、貧窮，還是家庭壓力？ 💬 "What risk did she face: dropout, early marriage, violence, poverty, or family pressure?"

  • 她在哪一刻開始不只是被幫助，而是開始幫助別人？ 💬 "When did she stop being only a beneficiary and start helping others?"

  • 她的姓名、地點、照片或細節能否公開？若不能，應如何匿名？ 💬 "Can her name, place, photo, or details be public? If not, how should we anonymize her?"

Q10. 這本書最不該漏掉什麼？(What This Book Must Not Miss)

中文提問概念：最後一題不是請他給漂亮結語，而是請他站在親歷者的位置，告訴作者：如果這本書只留下官方成就，會漏掉什麼？如果三十年後有人讀它，最需要理解世友、Nagaloka、Manuski 與這場運動的哪一個不容易說出口的真相？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "This is my last interview with you."
  • "If this book only records official achievements, it will miss something."
  • "From your life experience, what must this book not miss?"
  • "What is the difficult truth about Lokamitra, Nagaloka, Manuski, or this movement?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 哪一種寫法會讓您覺得「這不是真的」？ 💬 "What kind of writing would make you feel, 'This is not true'?"

  • 哪些內容可以寫，哪些最好不要寫？ 💬 "What can be written, and what should not be written?"

  • 如果三十年後的年輕領導者讀這本書，您希望他帶走哪一句話？ 💬 "If a young leader reads this thirty years from now, what one sentence should stay with them?"
