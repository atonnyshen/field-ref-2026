深度訪談大綱：Dhammachari Vivekratna（明辨之寶）

本大綱專為前 Nagaloka 總監 Vivekratna 設計。考量到現場訪談的流暢度，所有的英文提問已針對口說情境（Spoken English）進行優化。長句子已被拆解為 2 到 3 個簡短、直白且容易發音的短句。

💡 訪談小建議：發問時不用急，每一句短句說完可以稍微停頓，搭配眼神交流，這樣對話感會更自然。

第一部分：生命軌跡與信仰覺醒

Q1: 信仰的轉折與啟蒙 (The Turning Point)

中文提問概念：在您成為 Nagaloka 總監之前，是什麼契機讓您決定放下世俗軌道，全心投入安貝卡博士的運動？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "You have worked in Nagaloka for a long time."
  • "What made you choose this path?"
  • "Was there a turning point that changed your life?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 你還記得第一次遇見 Lokamitra 的場景嗎？ 💬 "Do you remember the first time you met Lokamitra? What did he say?"

  • 您的家人當時對您這個決定有什麼反應？ 💬 "How did your family feel about your decision?"

Q2: 法名「明辨之寶」的重量 (The Weight of the Name)

中文提問概念：您的法名意為「智慧辨察之寶」。在過去幾十年的社會工作中，這個名字對您有什麼意義？您曾在哪些困難時刻依靠它的力量？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "Your dharma name is Vivekratna. It means the Jewel of Wisdom."
  • "You have done social work for decades. How does this name help you?"
  • "In your hardest times, how did this name give you strength?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 在管理龐大機構時，您如何實踐這種「明辨」？ 💬 "When managing this big campus, how do you practice this wisdom?"

  • Lokamitra 曾如何評價您這方面的特質？ 💬 "What did Lokamitra say about this part of you?"

第二部分：與 Lokamitra 的日常相處與共事

Q3: 草創期的並肩作戰 (Grassroots Days in the 1990s)

中文提問概念：回顧 1990 年代剛在 Nagpur 開展工作時，條件一定很艱難。當時您與 Lokamitra 是如何互動與分工的？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "Let's talk about the 1990s in Nagpur."
  • "Things must be very tough back then."
  • "How did you and Lokamitra work together every day? How did you divide the work?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • Lokamitra 在日常生活中有沒有什麼執拗的小習慣？ 💬 "Did Lokamitra have any funny or stubborn daily habits?"

  • 當時你們最常在哪裡開會？ 💬 "Where did you usually have meetings back then?"

Q4: 決策背後的衝突與互補 (Conflicts and Complementarity)

中文提問概念：兩個懷抱強烈願景的人一起工作，必定會有意見相左。在建立 Nagaloka 的過程中，您們曾在哪些重大議題上有分歧？如何解決？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "Working closely together is not easy. You both have strong visions."
  • "Did you two have big arguments when building Nagaloka?"
  • "How did you solve the conflicts?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 當 Lokamitra 展現出西方人的急躁時，您是如何擔任橋樑的？ 💬 "When he was impatient, how did you balance him out?"

Q5: 挫折與不為人知的脆弱 (The Unseen Vulnerability)

中文提問概念：外界看 Lokamitra 總是充滿能量。但在這幾十年間，您是否曾見過他展現脆弱或沮喪的時刻（例如資金或政治壓力）？你們如何相互扶持？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "Lokamitra always looks very energetic to others."
  • "But have you ever seen him feel very frustrated or weak?"
  • "Maybe during money problems? How did you support each other?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 有沒有哪一次危機，讓你們覺得計畫幾乎要進行不下去了？ 💬 "Was there a time you felt the project might fail?"

  • 在這種時刻，佛法如何幫助你們？ 💬 "How did Buddhism help you in those dark moments?"

第三部分：Nagaloka 的建構與空間戒律

Q6: 荒地上的遠見 (From Wasteland to Sanctuary)

中文提問概念：當 Nagaloka 現址還是一片荒地時，您們是如何構想這裡的？從第一棟建築到現在，這座校園如何改變了達利特學生的心理？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "At first, Nagaloka was just an empty land."
  • "When you built the first building, what was your dream?"
  • "How does this beautiful campus change the minds of the students?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 第一棟建築落成的那天，您們的心情如何？ 💬 "How did you feel on the day the first building was finished?"

  • 「行佛像」當初選址有什麼故事？ 💬 "Is there a story behind the Walking Buddha statue?"

Q7: 戒律的熔爐 (The Crucible of Discipline)

中文提問概念：我遇到 8 個月培訓班的學生，作息極度嚴格（禁語、無手機、男女分流）。對於這些年輕人，這種高壓隔離環境的教育哲學是什麼？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "Last night, I talked to an 8-month training student."
  • "He told me the rules are very strict here. No phones, and early meditation."
  • "Why do you make the rules so strict?"
  • "What changes do you want to see in them after 8 months?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 有些學生一開始無法適應，您們如何引導？ 💬 "If students cannot follow the rules at first, how do you help them?"

Q8: 繁雜管理中的修行 (Social Work as Spiritual Practice)

中文提問概念：Maitreyanath 將管理宿舍視為修行。作為前總監，您是如何避免世俗的行政繁瑣磨損熱情，將其轉化為個人的菩薩道修行？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "Maitreyanath told me that being a dorm manager is his spiritual practice."
  • "As a Director, you had to manage money, people, and many problems."
  • "How did you turn this busy daily work into your own Buddhist practice?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 當機構越來越制度化時，如何保持最初的草根熱情？ 💬 "As the campus grows bigger, how do you keep your original passion?"

第四部分：未來的傳承與安貝卡願景

Q9: 世代差異與傳承 (The Next Generation)

中文提問概念：目前 Nagaloka 超過七成工作人員（如 Bhanu）都曾是學生。您認為這群年輕一代在理解「入世佛教」上，與你們這一代有什麼不同？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "Today, many staff here are your former students, like Bhanu."
  • "They are now running Nagaloka."
  • "Do you think they understand Buddhism differently from your generation?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • 您會擔心機構變得過於學術化，而失去與底層的連結嗎？ 💬 "Are you worried the school might become too academic and lose touch with the poor?"

Q10: 終極的期許與未竟之業 (The Ultimate Hope & Unfinished Business)

中文提問概念：回顧這幾十年，如果您現在與 Lokamitra 一起喝茶，您們心中「未竟之業」是什麼？對未來的最大期許是什麼？

🗣️ 口語英文版 (Spoken English)：

  • "Imagine you and Lokamitra are drinking tea together right now."
  • "Looking back, what is your biggest unfinished dream?"
  • "What is your biggest hope for the future of Nagaloka?"

📌 引申追問 (Follow-ups)：

  • Lokamitra 留給這個社群最不可替代的精神遺產是什麼？ 💬 "What is the most important spirit Lokamitra will leave for this community?"
